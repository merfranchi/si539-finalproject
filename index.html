Hi, I'm Meredith.

I am an avid listener of good music and enthusiastic concert-goer to get the full experience of my favorite bands. Unlike most people my age, I love '70s and '80s classic rock and have been lucky enough to see some of the most popular bands of those decades perform their timeless classics live. Check out all the concerts I've been to since I first started 10 years ago (there are some current names in the mix too!) to get a feel for what each experience was like and see which concerts I'm hoping to go to next to add to my archive!
